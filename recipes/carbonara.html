Poggies Carbonara

Authentic italian pasta dish made with Guanciale and Pecorino Romano

Ingredients

  • 300g Spaghetti
  • 100g Guanciale
  • 3 Eggs
  • 60g Pecorino Romano
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Bring pot of water to a boil and season generously with salt.
  2. Cut the guanciale in fine strips and begin to render the fat over low to medium heat.
  3. Seperate the yolks of 2 eggs from the eggwhite and combine them with a whole egg in a bowl.
  4. Finely grate the Pecorino and add it to the eggs together with some of the rendered fat from the guanciale.
  5. Cook the pasta according to package directions and drain, saving some pasta water for later.
  6. Add the eggmixture to the pasta and stir, adding pasta water to acquire the wanted consistency.
  7. Put the Guanciale on top and serve with freshly cracked black pepper and grated pecorino.
