Creamy lemon and chicken gnocchi

Gnochhi cooked in a creamy lemon sauce with chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g Gnocchi
  • 1 Lemon
  • 250ml Cream
  • 500g Parmesan
  • 200ml Chickenstock
  • 300g Chicken
  • 2x Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive oil 1tsp.
  • Butter 1tsp.
  • Parsley

Preparation

  1. Use a microplane to grate the zest of one lemon and finely mince two cloves of garlic.
  2. Cut the chicken and season with salt, pepper, paprika, thyme and half of the lemonzest.
  3. Heat a tsp. of olive oil in a pan and cook chicken over high heat, it doesnt have to be fully cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pan, add butter and gnocchi and cook until golden brown.
  5. Add garlic and lemonzest to the pan and cook for 30 more seconds.
  6. Add cream and chickenstock to the pan and lower the heat to medium, let simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Finally add back in the chicken and the juice of one half of a lemon and simmer 2-3 more minutes before adding in the grated parmesan.
  8. Finish with the parsley and salt and pepper to taste.
