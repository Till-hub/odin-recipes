Chili con carne

Spicy chili con carne with beans and corn.

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground beef
  • 500g Can of tomatoes
  • 1can Corn
  • 1can Kidneybeans
  • 2 Large Onions
  • 500g Stock
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Kethchup
  • Cumin
  • Carlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Cinnamon
  • Oregano
  • Dark chocolate

Preparation

  1. Slice onion in chunks or fine strips.
  2. Sear the ground beef in a big pot over high heat.
  3. Add onions to the pot and salt them to draw out moisture.
  4. When the ground beef got some colour and there is fond at the bottom of the pan, add all the spices, i just measure by heart but probably a tsp of everything atleast.
  5. Cook until fragrant and then add tomatoes and stock and scrape of the fond off the bottom of the pot. Add some dark chocolate for colour and a better mouthfeel.
  6. Lower heat to low-medium and simmer for 2 hours.
  7. 30 minutes before serving add the can of corn and beans.
  8. Serve over rice or topped with some cheddar and sourcream.
